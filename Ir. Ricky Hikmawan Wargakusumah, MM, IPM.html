Ir. Ricky Hikmawan Wargakusumah, MM, IPM

Senior Expert

  •        R. Hikmawan Wargakusumah goes by Ricky. He obtained his bachelor degree in Chemical Engineering from Institut Teknologi Bandung. He continued to obtain his master degree in Business Law at Universitas Padjadjaran and he is currently working on his doctorate. He is one of the pioneers and renewable energy fighter in Indonesia, specifically in developing biogas and biomass for electricity. In 2006, he founded PT Pasadena Engineering Indonesia (PT PEI) (PT Tekalumni Konsultan Holding company) and he has become the President Director until now. In 2007, he started with small-scale biogas projects from livestock and tofu industry in Java and Bali.
          He moved on to much bigger projects. By 2015, he developed 1 MW biomass power plant in Sumba, East Nusa Tenggara and 3 MW biogas power plant in Hanau, Central Kalimantan. He is one of the appointed experts who drafted Indonesia’s renewable energy legislation. He is the Chairman of BKKPII (The Chemical Engineering Chapter of the Indonesian Engineers Association) 2017 – 2021. He is also the Head of Renewable Energy in the Energy Department at KADIN (Indonesian Chamber of Commerce and Industry). His influence and expertise also pushed him to found ABgI (Indonesian Biogas Association), where he is now a member of the association’s advisory board.
